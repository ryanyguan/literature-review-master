# Corpus Triage

## Purpose

Turn a pile of papers into a usable research corpus.

## Corpus categories

Classify each paper.

```text
Tier 0 direct predecessor
Tier 1 close neighbour
Tier 2 methodological foundation
Tier 3 application context
Tier 4 broad background
Tier 5 discard or monitor
```

## Corpus log fields

Use these fields.

```text
Paper ID
Full citation
Year
Journal or venue
ABS rating if applicable
Field
Method
Evidence type
Relevance tier
Reading pass level
Key claim
Risk to current idea
Use in manuscript
Verification status
```

## Stream map

Cluster papers by intellectual stream, not by keyword alone. Possible streams include these.

```text
Theory and mathematical modelling
Game theory and strategic interaction
Optimisation, algorithms and decision science
Statistical modelling and econometrics
Bayesian and probabilistic modelling
Machine learning, deep learning, AI and agent systems
Operations, supply chain and service systems
Marketing, consumer research, advertising, branding and retailing
Economics, finance and risk
Information systems and digital platforms
Engineering, project management and infrastructure systems
Healthcare, public policy, education and social science applications
Sustainability, energy, climate and environmental systems
Qualitative, conceptual and theory-building streams
```

Add project-specific streams when the corpus does not fit the default taxonomy.

## Triage output

A good triage output gives a reading plan.

```text
Read immediately
Read later
Cite only if needed
Monitor for risk
Discard
```
