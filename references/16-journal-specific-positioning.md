# Mode H. Journal-Specific Literature Positioning

## Purpose

The same paper needs different related work for different target journal families. Mode H reframes the literature map without changing the underlying evidence.

## Required output

For each target journal family, produce this.

```text
Core literature stream
Must-cite papers
Bridge literature
Literature to downplay
Dangerous missing literature
Preferred gap language
Evidence-structure feasibility
Likely reviewer profile
```

## Examples by family

Operations, management science and decision science route. Prioritise operational decision, optimisation, stochastic modelling, queueing, service systems, supply-demand coordination, risk, algorithms, equilibrium analysis, computational experiments and managerial implications.

Marketing, consumer, advertising, branding and retailing route. Prioritise consumer response, demand-side behaviour, brand or advertising mechanisms, pricing, channels, retail format, omnichannel strategy, platform market design and marketing decision relevance.

Economics and finance route. Prioritise economic mechanism, market equilibrium, valuation, risk, liquidity, financing constraints, contracting, market microstructure, household finance, corporate finance or policy implications.

Information systems and digital-technology route. Prioritise digital technology, platform design, data infrastructure, information architecture, AI systems, human-AI interaction, algorithmic governance, organisational adoption and IT-enabled decision processes.

Statistics, econometrics, machine learning and AI route. Prioritise methodological novelty, estimation, prediction-to-decision value, uncertainty quantification, benchmark performance, interpretability, robustness and reproducibility.

Engineering, project management and infrastructure route. Prioritise project control, scheduling, risk propagation, contract design, systems engineering, reliability, uncertainty, safety, decision support and operational feasibility.

Healthcare, public policy, education and social science route. Prioritise the substantive problem community, policy mechanism, ethical implications, implementation constraints, measurement validity and evidence quality.

Interdisciplinary prestige route. Prioritise broad scientific relevance, mechanism generality, external importance, cross-field credibility and evidence discipline. Do not cite prestige outlets merely to raise perceived status.
