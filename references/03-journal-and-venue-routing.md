## Routing principle

The journal should be selected by the paper's central contribution, not by vocabulary.

A platform setting does not automatically make an information systems contribution.
A financial setting does not automatically make a finance contribution.
A retail context does not automatically make a retailing contribution.
An optimisation model does not automatically make an operations research contribution.
A consumer demand function does not automatically make a marketing contribution.
A machine-learning model does not automatically make a computer-science contribution.
A field context does not automatically make a project empirical.

## Primary routing questions

Ask these before recommending journals.

```text
What decision, phenomenon, theory, method or mechanism is central?
What literature would recognise the contribution as native rather than borrowed?
What evidence exists?
What is the paper's home discipline if all application labels are removed?
Which literature would supply the most dangerous reviewer?
What journal family expects this evidence structure?
```

## Common routing lanes

Operations and management science work may route to operations, decision science, management science, optimisation, information systems, marketing, engineering, supply chain, healthcare or sustainability outlets depending on the central mechanism.

Marketing, consumer, advertising, branding and retailing work may route to marketing, consumer research, retailing, psychology, management science, information systems or economics outlets depending on whether the contribution is behavioural, managerial, methodological, strategic or empirical.

Economics and finance work belongs in economics or finance journals only when the field's theory, market mechanism, institutional setting, asset-pricing logic, corporate-finance mechanism, risk structure or identification concern is central.

Information systems and digital-technology work belongs in IS outlets when the technology artefact, digital platform, data infrastructure, algorithmic governance, human-technology interaction or organisational use of information systems is central.

Statistics, econometrics, machine learning and AI work belongs in method outlets when the method itself is new, and in domain outlets when the method is a tool for a substantive contribution.

Engineering, project management, healthcare, public policy, education, sustainability and interdisciplinary work should be routed by the community that owns the problem, not merely by the modelling or empirical method used.

## Search priority rule

For each target journal family, build three citation streams.

```text
Must-cite stream, without which the paper looks uninformed.
Bridge stream, which connects the home discipline to the application or method.
Boundary stream, which should be acknowledged but not allowed to take over the paper.
```

## Rating caution

ABS 4* and 4 papers should not be cited only because they are highly rated. Cite them only when they directly shape the research question, method, theory, mechanism, empirical design or gap.
